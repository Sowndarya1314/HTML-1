XYZ College (Autonomous)

History

X.Y.Z. College that was started with 120 students has been identified as College with Potential for Excellence (CPE) by University Grant Commission in 2011 and has been Accredited as 'A' Grade Institution in the third cycle of reaccreditation by NAAC in 2013. Presently it has grown to offer 17 Under Graduate, 19 Post Graduate and 17 Research programmes with a student strength of about 5600, faculty strength of 263 and non-teaching staff of 105 members. The College is celebrating its Diamond Jubilee in February-2016.

X.Y.Z College, In fact, the youth are lucky enough to study at the temple of learning, located on the bank of river Cauvery. This heritage centre offers value based education focusing on gender equality & social justice, transfer the knowledge of research from lab to life, facilitate the widespread use of technology by rural populace at a resource outlay, promote extension activities by creating a network of service villages to mitigate the rural-urban divide, impart adequate skills to ensure economic independence, inculcate an aptitude for active participation in grass roots democratic governance, equip the stakeholders with innovative competence to become active agents for social change, to incorporate the sport of excellence in every academic and allied activity so that the quest for excellence is uneasing and sustained.

Our Vission Mission

VISION: As a citadel of higher learning offering quality education to downtrodden people.

MISSION: Orienting education to uplift rural youth. Empowering women by enhancing their literacy and employability.
